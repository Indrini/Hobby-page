All About the Kalimba

What is a Kalimba?

The kalimba is a unique musical instrument that consists of a wooden soundboard and metal keys. The metal keys, known as tines, are plucked to produce a magical sound. The origins of the instrument can be traced back to Africa and it was brought to the attention of the rest of the world by ethnomusicologist Hugh Tracey.

Why you should learn to play the kalimba

  • It's easy to play, carry and hold
  • They're reasonably cheap
  • It has a magical sound
  • It's calming and helps relieve stress
